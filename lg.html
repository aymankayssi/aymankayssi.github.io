Letter Grading

This page was created to support the transition from numeric grades to letter grades.

Translation Table

Use this table to obtain the letter equivalent of a numeric grade. 
-----------------------
Range ---> Letter Grade
-----------------------
 <60		F
 60		D	
 61-62		D+	
 63-65		C-	
 66-68		C	
 69-71		C+	
 72-74		B-	
 75-78		B	
 79-82		B+	
 83-86		A-	
 87-92		A	
 93-100		A+

Excel Formula

Copy the below formula and paste it in an Excel cell. It will give you the equivalent letter grade of the number in the cell to the left of it. 
=VLOOKUP(ROUND(INDIRECT("RC[-1]",0),0),{0,"F";60,"D";61,"D+";63,"C-";66,"C";69,"C+";72,"B-";75,"B";79,"B+";83,"A-";87,"A";93,"A+"},2,TRUE)
